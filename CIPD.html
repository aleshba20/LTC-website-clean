CIPD Level 3 Foundation Certificate in People Practice

Start Your HR Career with Confidence

About the Qualification

The CIPD Level 3 Foundation Certificate in People Practice is an internationally recognized UK qualification that builds essential knowledge and practical skills for HR, L&D, and people operations. It’s the perfect first step toward CIPD membership and a career in people practice.

Who Should Enroll?

Ideal for individuals beginning an HR/People career, working professionals seeking an accredited HR qualification, business owners and team leaders who manage people and performance, and graduates seeking a globally recognized HR credential. No prior HR experience is required—just curiosity and commitment.





Why CIPD at Leaders?

  • Approved CIPD Learning Partner: UK-accredited provider based in Doha.
  • Qualified CIPD Tutors: Decades of HR & L&D experience with practical insights.
  • Flexible Schedules: Weekday evenings and weekend options for working professionals.
  • Dedicated Tutor Support: Ongoing feedback, guidance, and resources throughout.
  • Career Coaching: CV help and interview preparation for graduates.
  • Modern Learning: Blended classroom + online live sessions with resources.




Course Details

  • Qualification: CIPD Level 3 Foundation Certificate in People Practice
  • Mode of Study: Blended learning (Classroom + Live Online)
  • Assessment: Written assignments and work-based projects (no exams)
  • Awarded By: Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (CIPD, UK)

Each unit is assessed through practical, work-related assignments that demonstrate your understanding and application of HR concepts in real scenarios.

What You’ll Learn (4 Core Units)

  • Business, Culture & Change in Context: How organizations work and how to manage change effectively.
  • Principles of Analytics: Use data and evidence to make smarter people decisions.
  • Core Behaviours for People Professionals: Professional ethics, values, and inclusive leadership behaviours.
  • Essentials of People Practice: Hands-on skills in recruitment, performance, and development.
