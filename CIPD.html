    CIPD Level 3 Foundation Certificate in People Practice

    Start Your HR Career with Confidence

    About the Qualification

    The CIPD Level 3 Foundation Certificate in People Practice is an internationally recognized UK qualification that builds essential knowledge and practical skills for HR, L&D, and people operations. It‚Äôs the perfect first step toward CIPD membership and a career in people practice.

    Who Should Enroll?

    Ideal for individuals beginning an HR/People career, working professionals seeking an accredited HR qualification, business owners and team leaders who manage people and performance, and graduates seeking a globally recognized HR credential. No prior HR experience is required just curiosity and commitment.

    The CIPD Level 3 qualification provides a practical introduction to Human Resources and People Practice for beginners. It covers core topics such as organizational culture, business fundamentals, people analytics, employee lifecycle support, and the key behaviours expected of HR professionals. The program suits those aiming to start roles like HR Assistant, HR Administrator, or L&D Coordinator. Most learners finish within six to twelve months and gain Foundation Membership of the CIPD upon completion.

    Course Details

    • Qualification: CIPD Level 3 Foundation Certificate in People Practice
    • Mode of Study: Blended learning (Classroom + Live Online)
    • Assessment: Written assignments and work-based projects (no exams)
    • Awarded By: Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (CIPD, UK)

    Each unit is assessed through practical, work-related assignments that demonstrate your understanding and application of HR concepts in real scenarios.

    What You‚Äôll Learn (4 Core Units)
