CMA in Doha, Qatar (Certified Management Accountant)

CMA Training Program in Doha, Qatar

Certified Management Accountant CMA (USA) is an internationally recognized qualification provided by IMA (USA). You can add a great value to your career by obtaining this prestigious qualification and get demanded by top companies all over the world. CMA happens to be the most convenient, economic and smart way to be a full-fledged management/cost accountant within a span of 9 months to 1 Year

Contact our counselors for free advice at +974 31101550

Why CMA?

  • International Recognition: CMA USA is recognized in Qatar as well as globally & exams can be taken in Qatar.
  • Short Duration Course: Duration of 9 months to 1 Year from the start date; however, students may prefer more preparation.
  • IMA's MoU with ICWAI: ICWAI (The Institute of Cost and Works Accountants off India) and IMA (Institute of Management Accountants).
  • Better Salary Packages: CMAs earn significantly higher salaries and compensation than noncertified counterparts.
  • Job Opportunities: Common job titles include Cost Accountant, Financial Analyst.
  • Faculty: Experienced faculty (5–10 years) from top US-based MNCs.
  • Exclusive online video lectures provided only by Professional Training Centre.

CMA Topic Guide

Part 1: Financial Planning, Performance, and Analytics

  • 15% External Financial Reporting Decisions.
  • 20% Planning, Budgeting, and Forecasting.
  • 20% Performance Management.
  • 15% Cost Management.
  • 15% Internal Controls.
  • 15% Technology and Analytics.

Part 2: Strategic Financial Management

  • 20% Financial Statement Analysis.
  • 20% Corporate Finance.
  • 25% Decision Analysis.
  • 10% Risk Management.
  • 10% Investment Decisions.
  • 15% Professional Ethics.
