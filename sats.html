SAT English Preparation Course

SAT English Preparation Course

Introduction:

At Leaders Training Centre, we provide specialized SAT English preparation designed to help students strengthen their reading, writing, and language skills. Our program focuses on critical reading strategies, grammar, vocabulary, and essay writing techniques to ensure students are well-prepared to achieve higher scores and excel in their SAT exams.





Course Details

  • Duration: Total of 24 hours
  • Format: Blended (online/in-person) interactive sessions with experienced instructors.
  • Schedule:
    • Option 1: One month-long Intensive course, 4 days a week from 6:20 to 7:50 PM and from 8:00 to 9:30 PM.
    • Option 2: One-to-one course any day, any time for a minimum of 10 hours.
    • Option 3: 2 month-long weekdays from 6:20 to 7:50 PM and from 8:00 to 9:30 PM.
    • Option 4: 2 month-long Fridays from 8:00 to 11:00 AM or 1:00 to 4:00 PM.
  • Class Size: Limited to 12 participants for personalized attention.

Key Benefits

  • Expert Instructors: Learn from experienced instructors.
  • Personalized Learning: Receive individual feedback and customized study plans.
  • Practice and Feedback: Opportunities for practice with constructive feedback.
  • Certification: Certificate awarded upon successful completion of the program.
