French & Spanish Language Courses in Qatar

Expand Your World with French & Spanish

Interactive Lessons, Conversation, Practice, Cultural Immersion

Learn French and Spanish at Leaders Training Center, Qatar. Whether for travel, study, business, or career advancement, our expert instructors guide you from beginner to advanced proficiency through structured, interactive lessons aligned to CEFR levels (A1–C1).





Why Learn French & Spanish?

French is spoken by 300M+ people in 50+ countries; Spanish has 480M+ native speakers and is the second-most spoken language worldwide. Mastering these languages opens opportunities in global business, education, travel, and cultural exchange.

  • Improve career prospects in global companies.
  • Enhance communication for travel, study, or work.
  • Connect with diverse cultures and communities.
  • Strengthen your CV with respected language certificates.

Course Objectives

  • Develop reading, writing, listening, and speaking from A1 to C1.
  • Build confidence in real-life and professional conversations.
  • Enhance cultural awareness and global understanding.
  • Prepare for DELF/DALF (French) and DELE (Spanish) international exams.
  • Follow CEFR-aligned, interactive lesson design.

What You’ll Learn

🇫🇷 French Language Modules

  • Beginner (A1–A2): greetings, daily expressions, simple conversations.
  • Intermediate (B1–B2): grammar, tenses, vocabulary expansion, cultural topics.
  • Advanced (C1): professional communication, debate, DELF/DALF prep.
  • French for Business: email writing, negotiation terms, workplace etiquette.

🇪🇸 Spanish Language Modules

  • Beginner (A1–A2): everyday phrases, pronunciation, basic structures.
  • Intermediate (B1–B2): grammar, past/future tenses, conversational fluency.
  • Advanced (C1): professional communication, DELE exam readiness.
  • Spanish for Business: business vocabulary, presentations, cultural awareness.

Learning materials and activities emphasize role-plays, dialogues, and practical communication.

Who Should Attend

  • Students and professionals seeking global education or careers.
  • Travel enthusiasts and expatriates living in Qatar.
  • Business executives working with international clients.
  • Anyone wishing to learn a new language or enhance communication.

Duration & Certification

  • Duration: 8–12 weeks per level
  • Mode: Classroom / Online / Hybrid
  • Certification: French or Spanish Language Certificate from Leaders Training Center (Gold-Accredited).

Benefits

  • Gain fluency in two globally influential languages.
  • Strengthen your profile for international study and work.
  • Improve communication and cultural understanding.
  • Learn from qualified, native-speaking instructors using immersive methods.

Why Leaders Training Center

  • Gold-accredited institute with internationally recognized programs.
  • Qualified native instructors and small class sizes.
  • Interactive learning with role-plays, dialogues, and multimedia.
  • Flexible schedules: weekday, weekend, and online batches.
  • Proven exam-prep support for DELF/DALF & DELE.
