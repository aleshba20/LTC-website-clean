CIA® (Certified Internal Auditor)

CIA is the only globally recognized certification for internal auditors. Becoming a Certified Internal Auditor® (CIA®) is a strong way to demonstrate competence in internal audit. 📄 Download CIA Brochure (PDF)

Why Choose CIA?

Earn More By Becoming A CIA

  • CIAs earn on average QR 150,000 (US$38,000) more annually than those without a CIA certification.
  • CIA helps you get to the interview table for internal audit roles.

Entry Requirements

Students Must Have Completed A Bachelor’s Degree

Those without a bachelor’s degree can still get entry into CIA if they have:

  • Two years' post-secondary education and five years verified experience in internal audit or its equivalent.
  • OR seven years' verified experience in internal audit or its equivalent.

Exams

  • 125 MCQs for Part 1 – 2.5 hours.
  • 100 MCQs for Part 2 – 2.0 hours.
  • 100 MCQs for Part 3 – 2.0 hours.

Exams are conducted in Pearson VUE centres across Qatar.

Training Structure

  • The International Accounting Standards Board (IASB) and the regulatory framework.
  • Video recorded lectures for each part.
  • Official CIA study text and question bank.
  • Special tailor-made notes for our students.
  • Practice questions solved in class for each part.

Syllabus

Part 1 – Internal Audit Basics

  • Mandatory Guidance (35–45)
  • Internal Control / Risk (25–35) – Awareness Level (A)
  • Conducting Internal Audit Engagements – Tools & Techniques (25–35)

Part 2 – Internal Audit Practice

  • Managing the Internal Audit Function (40–50)
  • Managing Individual Engagements (40–50)
  • Fraud Risks and Controls (5–15)

Part 3 – Internal Audit Knowledge

  • Governance / Business Ethics (5–15)
  • Risk Management (10–20) – Proficiency Level (P)
  • Organizational Structure / Processes & Risks (15–25)
  • Communication (5–10)
  • Management / Leadership Principles (10–20)
  • IT / Business Continuity (15–25)
  • Financial Management (10–20)
  • Global Business Environment (0–10)

CIA in Qatar

  • CIA is in high demand in Qatar and is widely preferred for internal audit roles.
  • An active local IIA Chapter supports members and the profession.
  • Exams are held in Qatar at various Pearson VUE Approved Centres.
