    CPP – Certified Purchasing Professional

    Certified Purchasing Professional (CPP)

    Develop Professional Purchasing & Supply Chain Skills

    The Certified Purchasing Professional course takes you through a training method that is quite different from the usual classroom theory sessions. The programme makes use of study methods including case studies, presentations, videos, and group exercises with the objective of enhancing the learning experience and making the candidates capable enough to imply the learnings in their professional life.

    📄 View CPP Brochure (PDF)

    Course Objectives

    The CPP certification intends to help the candidates widen their knowledge in the purchasing sector. The course aims at helping you:

    • Learn about the importance of planning and executing successful price negotiation
    • Enhance your performance by training you on the efficiency criteria and operational standards.
    • Understand the dynamics and challenges involved in purchasing and negotiation.
    • Ensure improved proficiency in purchasing and supply chain practices
    • Acquire the skills required to encounter and resolve complex purchasing and supply chain challenges in the real world

    Target Audience

    CPP is designed for entry-level, midlevel and senior-level professionals who are desired about levelling up their career in purchasing, supply chain management or logistics sector.

    Target Competencies

    • Purchasing and strategy development
    • International Purchasing
    • Purchasing Evaluation
    • Off Shore and Domestic Purchasing
    • Purchasing Contracts
    • Purchasing Cycles and Systems

    Why Choose CPP Certification?

    The Certified Purchasing Professional (CPP) certification helps professionals develop practical purchasing, procurement, negotiation, and supply chain management skills required in modern organizations.

    This course is designed to improve strategic purchasing capabilities, operational efficiency, and decision-making skills while preparing professionals to handle real-world procurement challenges confidently.

    • Industry-focused practical learning approach
    • Hands-on purchasing and negotiation strategies
    • Improved procurement planning and contract management skills
    • Enhanced supply chain and purchasing knowledge
    • Suitable for beginners and experienced professionals
