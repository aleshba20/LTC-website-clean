CIPS Procurement & Supply Qualifications

Progress to MCIPS Chartered with Leaders Training Centre – Doha, Qatar

Why CIPS with Leaders?

Globally Recognised, Career-Boosting, Practical

CIPS (The Chartered Institute of Procurement & Supply) is the world’s leading body for procurement and supply professionals. CIPS qualifications are recognised across every industry and geography. Advancing through the levels can lead to the MCIPS Chartered designation the benchmark of professional excellence in procurement and supply.

  • Internationally recognised qualifications and membership pathway to MCIPS.
  • Structured levels (L2–L6) aligned to the Global Standard and credit-based learning.
  • Access to study resources, exam masterclasses, and a global professional community.

CIPS Qualification Levels (L2 → L6)

Start at the level that matches your experience and progress towards the MCIPS Chartered designation. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

🎯

Level 2 — Certificate

Entry level for newcomers and cross-functional managers. Five core modules (18 credits total) introduce procurement, operations, stakeholders, technology, and inventory/logistics.

📈

Level 3 — Advanced Certificate

Build confidence and employability. Four core modules (24 credits) + one elective (6 credits) covering environments, ethics, contract admin, team dynamics, and logistics.

🧭

Level 4 — Diploma

The main entry to Diploma route. Eight core modules (60 credits) including scope/influence (12 cr), commercial contracting, negotiation, supplier relationships, asset management, and practice.

🛡️

Level 5 — Advanced Diploma

Emphasis on risk, contracts, legal implications, and performance. Five core modules (42 credits) + three electives (18 credits). Leads to Associate Member grade on completion.

🏅

Level 6 — Professional Diploma

Strategic leadership, global policy, and complex problem-solving. Four core modules (42 credits) + three electives (18 credits). Final step before applying for MCIPS Chartered.

📜

Path to MCIPS Chartered

Complete Diploma (L4) → Advanced Diploma (L5) → Professional Diploma (L6). Apply to upgrade to MCIPS Chartered on completion.
