Project Management Professional (PMP)

Globally Recognized Project Management Certification

The Project Management Professional (PMP) certification is one of the most prestigious and globally recognized project management credentials. It validates your ability to lead and direct projects, demonstrates your competency to employers, and enhances your career opportunities with higher salaries and worldwide recognition.

Why PMP?

  • Get an internationally recognized project management certification at affordable cost.
  • PMP certification looks great on a resume – use the three letters PMP next to your name.
  • Earn higher salary by being PMP certified.
  • Become part of a wide community across the world.
  • PMP certification demonstrates higher competency in Project Management and knowledge of best practices.

Right Certification For You?

  • Anyone who wants the freedom to work anywhere in the world.
  • PMP certification is globally recognized and highly sought-after (over 750,000 certified professionals worldwide).
  • Helps professionals showcase their skills and expertise to employers worldwide.
  • Be a distinguished Project Manager!

Eligibility Criteria

To Apply For PMP Certification, You Must Have one of The Following:

  • A secondary degree (associate’s degree, high school diploma, or equivalent), with 5 years of project management experience, 7,500 hours leading projects, and 35 hours of project management education.
  • A 4-year bachelor’s degree or equivalent, with 3 years of project management experience, 4,500 hours leading projects, and 35 hours of project management education.

Duration & Preparation

  • Three Class formats: Weekday Evenings (12 classes) or Friday Evenings (8 classes).
  • Printed and digital course materials, including PMP study text and practice questions, provided free of cost.
  • Expert trainers with proven track records support you in application, exam strategy, and preparation.
  • End-to-end support to ensure you pass the PMP exam on your first attempt.

How to Complete?

Step 01: Registration

  • Enroll with us and get access to the course materials and practice resources.

Step 02: Preparation

  • Attend classes at the Professional Training Centre.

Step 03: Examination

  • Register with PMI and sit for the exam at Prometric exam centers in Qatar.

Exam & Schedule

The Exam

  • 200 multiple-choice questions, computer-based, approximately 4 hours duration.

Maintaining PMP Certification

  • Valid for 3 years from the date of certification.
  • Renewal requires completing continuing education requirements (no need to retake the exam).

How to Apply

  • Register with PMI and create an account.
  • Apply for PMI Membership and complete the online application.
  • Submit the application.
  • Upon acceptance, schedule your exam.

Fees & Resources

  • Certificate accepted by the Project Management Institute (PMI).
  • 35 hours of live lectures and 31 hours of high-quality video lectures.
  • Hard & soft copy of training material including questions and notes.
  • Free re-training for non-passing students.
  • Refreshments and stationery included.
  • Professional Development Units (PDUs) awarded.
  • Practice questions embedded within the program.
  • 10% discount for early registrations (*conditions apply).
