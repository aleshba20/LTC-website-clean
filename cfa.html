CFA® Program Registration & Exam

  • The CFA® Program includes exams in three levels, to be passed sequentially.
  • Candidates may register for only one exam at a time and must await results before the next level.
  • Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 exam formats and windows are set by CFA Institute.

CFA® Exam Question Formats

  • Level 1: Multiple-choice focused on foundational tools and concepts.
  • Level 2: Item sets emphasizing application and analysis for valuation.
  • Level 3: Essay (constructed response) and item sets on portfolio management.

Charter Requirements

To be awarded the CFA® charter you must:

  • Pass all three CFA® exam levels.
  • Accrue the required qualified investment work experience.
  • Become a member of CFA® Institute.
  • Pledge to abide by the CFA® Institute Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct.
