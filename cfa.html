CFA¬Æ Program Registration & Exam

  • The CFA¬Æ Program includes exams in three levels. These three exams must be passed sequentially as one of the requirements for earning the CFA charter.
  • Candidates may register for only one exam at a time and must wait to receive their results before registering for the next level.
  • Level 1 is conducted twice a year, in June and December, while Levels 2 and 3 are administered just once a year in June.

The CFA¬Æ exams include three types of question format.

  • The Level 1 exam consists of multiple choice questions only and covers Knowledge and comprehension of fundamental investment tools and concepts.
  • The Level 2 exam consists of case study item set questions only and covers Application and analysis focused on asset valuation.
  • The Level 3 exam morning session comprises essay questions and the afternoon session is composed of item set questions and covers Synthesis and evaluation of client needs and portfolio management issues.

Charter Requirements:

In order to be awarded the CFA¬Æ Charter you must:

  • Pass all levels of CFA¬Æ Program.
  • Have four years of qualified investment work experience.
  • Become a member of CFA¬Æ Institute.
  • Pledge to adhere to the CFA¬Æ Institute Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct.
