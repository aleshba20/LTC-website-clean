    CFA¬Æ Program Registration & Exam

    • The CFA¬Æ Program includes exams in three levels, to be passed sequentially.
    • Candidates may register for only one exam at a time and must await results before the next level.
    • Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 exam formats and windows are set by CFA Institute.

    CFA¬Æ Exam Question Formats

    • Level 1: Multiple-choice focused on foundational tools and concepts.
    • Level 2: Item sets emphasizing application and analysis for valuation.
    • Level 3: Essay (constructed response) and item sets on portfolio management.
