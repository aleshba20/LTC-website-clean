Admin & Secretarial Skills

Certificate in Admin & Secretarial Skills:

Employers all over the world, including Qatar pay a premium for people who have good administration skills. A recent survey done by the Foundation for the Development of Professionals, Austria concluded that employers are ready to pay extra for the following skills:





Why is this Course one of our most popular course?

  • The certificate in Admin & Secretarial Skills is very popular because it develops skills that are required in every organization and in almost all positions that you work in.
  • During the course, we will give you the basics of the skills included in the syllabus and also provide you a large number of resources to continue learning these skills on your own.
  • This certificate will be a starting point; using it, you will continue to develop your skills in your working career.
  • The course is ideal for people who want to get a feel of areas like marketing, finance, accounting, human resources, etc. After taking the course you will be more clear about the benefits of each of these areas and you can choose the one which is good for you.

Course Outline

The syllabus for Certificate of Admin & Secretarial Skills includes the following areas:

  • Business English and Correspondence.
  • Understanding behavior.
  • Basic bookkeeping and accounting.
  • Training on software used by administrators (MS Word, MS Outlook, MS Publisher, MS Excel, etc.).
  • Time Management.
  • Stress Management.
  • Other admin related skills.

Duration & Fee

Duration

The Certificate in Admin & Secretarial Skills is a 24-hour course. The course will be completed in a period of six weeks. There is one class every week i.e., on Friday from 02:00 pm till 06:00 pm.

Fees

The standard fees for the course is QR 2,500. The following discounts are applicable:

  • Cash payment discount: 10%. If paying in installments, the students have to pay the fee in two equal installments. One has to be paid before the start of the course and one after the course ends.
  • Early registration discount: 10%.

The above mentioned fees include:

  • Registration fees.
  • Tuition fees.
  • Study material charges.
  • Exam fees.
