Public Speaking & Leadership Communication

Transform How You Speak, Lead, and Influence

Course Overview

At Leaders Training Centre (Doha, Qatar), we don’t just train—we transform professionals into confident, inspiring leaders. Whether you’re a manager, entrepreneur, or aspiring leader, this course builds the confidence, clarity, and communication power to lead effectively in today’s competitive world.

Expect hands-on workshops, real-life speaking practice, and actionable leadership frameworks—backed by expert coaches, flexible batches, and a recognized certificate of achievement.

What You Will Learn

  • Leadership Development: Build self-awareness and emotional intelligence; lead with vision; manage workplace challenges; adapt your leadership style to diverse teams.
  • Public Speaking Excellence: Master stage presence and body language; overcome fear; structure persuasive talks; use storytelling to inspire; speak confidently in meetings and conferences.
  • Communication for Leaders: Active listening and feedback; negotiation and conflict resolution; persuasive executive presence; cultural awareness for multicultural teams.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6–8 weeks (18–24 total training hours).
  • Format: Interactive workshops, speeches, simulations, and coached practice.
  • Schedule: Evening & weekend batches available for working professionals.
  • Class Size: Capped for high feedback and individual coaching.
  • Outcome: Certificate in Leadership & Public Speaking.

Key Benefits

  • Speak with clarity, persuasion, and presence.
  • Strengthen leadership confidence and influence.
  • Lead teams more effectively and improve morale.
  • Build a strong personal brand as a communicator.

Who Should Attend?

  • Managers, team leaders, and supervisors seeking greater impact.
  • Entrepreneurs and business owners who must inspire teams and clients.
  • Professionals preparing for promotions, panels, or public roles.
  • Students and graduates who want to boost confidence and employability.
