NEBOSH International Diploma (IDIP)

Strategic HSE leadership qualification in Doha

About the Qualification

The NEBOSH IDIP is an advanced, globally respected diploma for health and safety professionals. It focuses on strategic management, leadership, safety culture, and risk control across complex operations, and is trusted by employers and regulators worldwide.

Why Choose Leaders Training Centre (Qatar)

  • Gold-standard learning partner delivering globally recognised NEBOSH programs.
  • Expert tutors with real-world HSE leadership and management experience.
  • Flexible study: classroom, live online, or blended learning options.
  • Comprehensive support: materials, interactive sessions, mock exams, and 1:1 guidance.

Course Structure (2023 specification)

  • DI1 – Workplace Health & Safety Principles: strategic HSE management and leadership, safety culture and human factors, auditing, performance, and risk control.
  • DI2 – Controlling Workplace Health Issues: occupational health & hygiene, control of physical/psychological hazards, wellbeing, ergonomics, and health surveillance.
  • DI3 – Controlling Workplace Safety Issues: fire and explosion, construction/electrical/transport safety, process safety management, and hazard control systems.

Who Should Enrol

  • HSE Managers, Officers, and Supervisors aiming for senior roles.
  • Health & safety consultants and auditors.
  • Professionals targeting strategic leadership in safety management.
  • Those preparing for advanced professional memberships (e.g., IOSH).

Assessment & Certification

  • Assignment-based evaluations plus a practical workplace application project (no traditional written exams).
  • On completion of all units, the NEBOSH International Diploma certificate is issued from the UK.

Recognition & Memberships

  • Recognised across 130+ countries by employers and regulators.
  • Meets academic requirements toward professional memberships such as IOSH (CMIOSH pathway) and IIRSM (Full Membership).

Career Opportunities

  • HSE Manager / Senior Safety Advisor
  • Safety & Compliance Director
  • Occupational Health & Risk Manager
  • Health & Safety Consultant / Corporate HSE Trainer / Safety Auditor

Holding the NEBOSH IDIP strengthens progression to senior leadership and enhances salary potential in Qatar and globally.

Entry Requirements

  • NEBOSH IGC or equivalent is recommended but not mandatory.
  • Suitable for experienced practitioners taking on strategic HSE responsibility.
