ACCA (Association Of Certified Chartered Accountant)

ACCA in Doha, Qatar

The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) is the world’s largest and fastest-growing professional accounting body, established in 1904. With over 500,000 members and students across 190 countries, ACCA represents excellence in accounting, finance, and business management.

Through a global network of 91 offices and over 8,500 approved employers, ACCA-qualified professionals are employed by leading multinational companies and top global brands. The ACCA Qualification equips students with the latest technical, strategic, and analytical skills to excel in today’s dynamic financial environment.

At Leaders Training Center, Qatar, we are proud to be an ACCA Gold Approved Learning Partner, recognized for delivering high-quality tuition, expert mentoring, and outstanding student support. Our ACCA programs develop well-rounded finance professionals who can ensure financial integrity and drive organizational growth.

Key Benefits

  • ACCA members can sign audit reports and work as licensed auditors.
  • Top global brands, MNCs, PLCs, LTDs employ ACCA students and members worldwide.
  • You can travel to work around the world with ACCA (Approved in 190 countries).
  • Certificates are awarded at every level of qualification.
  • Earn a degree from University from University.
  • With CPD (Continuing Professional Development), ACCA members keep up-to-date with business needs.

Who Can Join?

There are no formal entry requirements for ACCA. This is open to anyone wanting to increase their knowledge and skills in Finance. You can start at any level that suits you best.

  • Advance level / Ordinary level / CBSE / ICOM → Start from first paper.
  • BSc, BBA, MBA Finance, HND → Exempted from 4–9 papers.
  • AAT complete → Exempted from 1–3 papers.
  • CA → Exempted from first 9 papers.
  • Other professional qualifications → Exempted from 4–9 papers.

Learn more about exemptions on the ACCA official website (Exemptions Calculator) .

Gold Accreditation

You will have a 92% guaranteed pass if you do the following:

  • Attend 92% of the classes or above.
  • Pass the 3 mock exams in class.

If you still cannot pass, you can attend the next session for free. If conditions aren’t met, a 50% fee applies for retaking.
