NEBOSH Level 6 Environmental Management

Degree-level qualification delivered in Doha

About the Qualification

The NEBOSH Level 6 Qualification in Environmental Management is a globally recognised, degree-level programme (SCQF 10 / RQF 6) valued by employers seeking environmental assurance, sustainability leadership and strategic risk management. It provides a strong platform for advanced study and professional membership.

Why Choose This Qualification

  • Globally recognised; mapped to SCQF 10 / RQF 6 (degree-level).
  • Highly valued for environmental assurance and sustainability leadership.
  • Solid platform for MSc progression and professional memberships.

Who Should Enrol

  • Environmental management professionals deepening expertise.
  • HSE practitioners with environmental responsibilities.
  • Regional professionals (Qatar / Middle East) seeking international standards.

What You Will Learn

  • Principles/drivers of sustainability and building an EMS (e.g., ISO 14001 alignment).
  • Identifying and controlling environmental aspects (air/water, waste, contaminated land).
  • Regulatory frameworks, multilateral treaties, leadership and continuous improvement.
  • Applying knowledge in real workplaces to influence standards and practice.

Qualification & Recognition

On successful completion you are awarded the NEBOSH Level 6 Qualification in Environmental Management (formerly International in Environmental Management) and may use the designatory letters IEnvDipNEBOSH. The mapping is SCQF Level 10 (equivalent to RQF Level 6, i.e., full undergraduate honours degree level).

Benefits

For You

  • Enhanced credibility, specialist knowledge and career progression.
  • Pathways into environmental/sustainability management and consultancy.
  • Improved eligibility for memberships (e.g., SIIRSM, IEMA) and further study.

For Your Organisation

  • Alignment with ISO 14001 and recognised standards; reduced risk and liability.
  • Demonstrated environmental excellence and social responsibility across sectors.

Entry Requirements & Assessment

  • No formal prerequisite; prior Certificate-level knowledge is recommended.
  • Assessment: written examination (Unit ED1) and workplace-based assignment (Unit IDEM2).

Why Study with Leaders Training Centre (Doha)

  • Delivered by experienced regional and international EHS professionals.
  • Flexible classroom, blended or online options for busy professionals in Qatar.
  • Localised support tailored to construction, oil & gas and industrial sectors.
