NEBOSH International General Certificate (IGC)

What is NEBOSH IGC?

The NEBOSH IGC is a globally respected qualification in occupational health and safety. It equips learners to identify, assess, and control workplace risks across sectors such as construction, oil & gas, manufacturing, logistics, education, and corporate environments.
