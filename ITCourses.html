Power BI Training in Qatar

Learn to build interactive reports and dashboards using Power BI Desktop and Power BI Service. This hands-on course covers data modeling with DAX, Power Query (M), relationships, measures, visuals, and publishing/sharing best practices for teams. Ideal for analysts, accountants, and anyone who wants to turn raw data into clear business insights.

  • Import, transform, and clean data in Power Query
  • Design star schemas and write core DAX measures
  • Create dashboards, bookmarks, and drillthrough
  • Publish to Power BI Service and manage workspaces

  • Excel: functions, charts, PivotTables, data cleanup
  • Word: formatting, styles, templates, long documents

Video Editing Course

Learn the full editing workflow for YouTube, social media, and corporate videos. We cover timeline editing, transitions, color correction, title design, audio cleanup, and export settings. Tracked using real projects and assets so you build a portfolio as you learn.

  • Cutting, trimming, multi-track editing
  • Color correction & grading fundamentals
  • Audio mixing, noise reduction, music/VO
  • Export presets for web and broadcast

Photography & Photo Editing

A practical course for beginners and content creators. Master camera basics (exposure triangle, focus, lenses), composition, lighting, and post-processing with Lightroom/Photoshop. Build a mini-portfolio across portrait, product, and outdoor shoots.

  • Manual mode: ISO, shutter speed, aperture
  • Lighting: natural vs. continuous/flash basics
  • Editing workflow: RAW, color, retouching
  • Export & delivery for social and print
