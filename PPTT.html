    Why PPTT with Leaders Training Centre?

    Practical, Modern, Classroom-Focused Training

    PPTT refers to Professional or Preparing to Teach / Pre-Primary Teachers Training. It equips educators with the practical skills, teaching methodologies, learner psychology, and classroom strategies needed to deliver effective learning experiences. The program suits aspiring teachers, school assistants, vocational trainers, and professionals who want to enter the teaching or training field.

    The course covers learner psychology, lesson planning, instructional design, classroom management, assessment techniques, and inclusive teaching. It prepares participants to teach confidently in schools, training institutes, corporate environments, and adult learning centers.
