Train the Trainer — UK Level 4 ELP

Train the Trainer (UK Level 4 ELP)

Internationally benchmarked instructional skills for trainers, teachers, and facilitators

The Train the Trainer program at Leaders Training Centre is a professional development pathway for corporate trainers, educators, coaches, and consultants who want to design and deliver engaging learning with confidence. The course aligns with UK awarding‑body standards and Qatari recognition, ensuring strong global credibility.





Program Highlights

  • Comprehensive Practical Training: Hands‑on sessions to master facilitation, presentation, and assessment.
  • International Certification: Pathways to HABC Level 4 TTT or OTHM Level 4 ELP — both OFQUAL‑approved.
  • Multiple Certificates: Leaders TTT certificate (attested in Qatar) plus a UK awarding‑body credential.
  • Flexible Tracks: Options to focus on corporate training, education, leadership coaching, or soft skills.
  • Career Support: Guidance on attestation procedures and professional development.




About Leaders

Established in 2019, Leaders Training Centre partners with global awarding bodies to deliver quality programs in Management, Health & Safety, Accountancy, and Languages. We keep class sizes small and instruction practical for measurable results.

Who Should Enroll

  • Corporate trainers and HR/L&D professionals
  • School and university teachers
  • Coaches, consultants, and public speakers
  • Workshop facilitators and subject‑matter experts

Delivery & Support

  • Blended, weekend, and evening schedules suitable for working professionals
  • Practical micro‑teaches with feedback mapped to awarding‑body rubrics
  • E‑learning portal access: templates, checklists, and exemplar lesson plans
  • WhatsApp/email support and mock assessments

Learning Outcomes

  • Plan outcome‑based sessions using adult learning principles
  • Deliver engaging training using facilitation, questioning, and presentation skills
  • Assess learning with valid tools and constructive feedback
  • Evaluate programs and refine delivery for continuous improvement

Certificates & Recognition

  • Leaders Training Centre — Train the Trainer Certificate (attested by Qatar Chamber & MoFA)
  • HABC Level 4 Train the Trainer (TTT) or OTHM Level 4 ELP (Professional Practice in Teaching & Training)
  • Both HABC and OTHM are OFQUAL‑regulated awarding organisations in the UK
